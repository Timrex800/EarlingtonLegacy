# Earlington Legacy Initiative (ELI)

Official website repository for the Earlington Legacy Initiative NPC.

The Earlington Legacy Initiative, established on December 1st, 2025, is a non-profit organization (NPC No: 2025/931583/08) dedicated to transforming Earlington Secondary School into a center of advanced learning and innovation.

We integrate cutting-edge tools, sponsored by Google Non-Profit, into the curriculum to bridge the digital divide and prepare students for the modern world. This includes high-speed 1GBPS fibre connectivity, AI training, robotics, drone programs, interactive learning boards, and future plans for tablets and secure WiFi.

Visit the live site: https://www.earlingtonlegacy.org.za/
